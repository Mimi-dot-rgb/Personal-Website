Brainwave Analysis using Machine Learning with Muse Headset and Splunk

This research paper explores the analysis of brainwaves using machine learning techniques to predict activities or mental states. The study combines the utilization of the Muse EEG headset and the Splunk platform for data collection, preprocessing, model building, and real-time prediction. The objective is to develop a machine learning system that accurately classifies educational activities based on EEG signals from the Muse headset. Additionally, the research investigates the effectiveness of the approach in differentiating between active and passive learning activities.
